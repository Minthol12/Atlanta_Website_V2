Essential resources, forms, and digital access instructions for students and parents.

All athletes must have a current physical on file before participating in practices or games.

Download Physical Form MSHSAA Handbook

Follow these steps to access your student portal and grades.

  • Open Chrome and go to the Atlanta C-3 homepage.
  • Click the 'Lumen' link under the Student tab.
  • Use your lunch number as your username.

Students can check out up to 3 books at a time for a period of two weeks. Digital e-books are available through the Sora app using your school credentials.
