Documentation Categories

Campus Wi-Fi Setup

  1. Open Wi-Fi settings on your device.
  2. Select ANC-Student.
  3. Login with your Student ID and Portal Password.
  4. If prompted, select "Trust" for the certificate.

Account Recovery

If you've forgotten your password, please visit the Media Center during advisory or use the automated reset tool on the student portal.

Common Threats

Phishing: Be wary of emails asking for login credentials. Check the sender domain carefully.

DDoS: Large scale traffic attacks meant to slow down networks. If the internet is crawling, IT is likely mitigating one.

Security Best Practices

  1. Use 12+ character passwords with symbols.
  2. Never share your student login with anyone.
  3. Log out of public computers in the lab before leaving.
